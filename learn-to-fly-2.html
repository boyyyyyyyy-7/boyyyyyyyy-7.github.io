Learn to Fly 2

About Learn to Fly 2

🎮 Bigger, Faster, Farther

The determined penguin is back with better gear and even more determination! Learn to Fly 2 takes everything that made the original great and expands it. Destroy snowmen, reach new distances, and unlock massive upgrades.

The sequel introduces new modes including Classic, Arcade, and Story mode, giving you plenty of ways to play. With improved physics and a massive list of achievements, your flight journey has never been more rewarding.

🎯 Destruction and Distance

It's not just about how far you go anymore—now you can smash through obstacles for extra cash! The shop is deeper than ever, featuring complex gliders, powerful boosts, and durable dummies to test your speed.

The medal system rewards you for specific challenges, giving you clear goals to aim for beyond just flying right. Use these medals to unlock special bonuses in the shop.

💡 Mastering the Skies

Invest in payload items early to increase your weight and momentum for smashing through snowmen. Watch out for the "Perfect Glide" angle which maximizes your lift-to-drag ratio.

Experiment with different glider and rocket combinations. Sometimes a slower, more stable flight earns more than a chaotic, high-speed burst that ends in a water-wreck.
