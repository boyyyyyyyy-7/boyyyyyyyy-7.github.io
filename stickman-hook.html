About Stickman Hook

Swing Physics Mastery

Stickman Hook is all about momentum and timing. You're a stickman swinging through levels using grappling hooks. The physics feel incredible—each swing builds speed, and releasing at the right moment sends you soaring through the air.

What makes it addictive is how simple it looks but how much skill it actually takes. Miss a hook point and you're falling. Release too early and you don't make the distance. But nail that perfect sequence of swings and you're blazing through levels like Spider-Man.

One-Touch Controls

Tap to hook onto attachment points, release to let go. That's it. The challenge comes from reading the level ahead and timing your swings to maintain speed without crashing into obstacles.

Each level adds new twists—moving platforms, bouncy surfaces, speed boosters. You need to chain swings together smoothly while adjusting mid-air to avoid hazards.

Getting Past Tough Levels

Don't just hook and release randomly. Watch the rhythm of the level. Some sections need fast swings, others need careful positioning. Build momentum in safe areas, then use that speed to clear difficult gaps.

If you're stuck, try different release points. Sometimes swinging lower or higher changes your trajectory just enough to clear obstacles you kept hitting.
