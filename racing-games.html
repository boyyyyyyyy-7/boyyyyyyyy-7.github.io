Why play racing games at school?

Racing games are perfect for a quick break between classes: a single run takes a minute or two, the controls are simple (usually just the arrow keys), and there's nothing to install. Because every game here is built in HTML5 and runs entirely in your browser, they load instantly and won't trip the download blockers on a school network or a managed Chromebook.

If you want pure chaos, Drive Mad turns simple obstacle courses into physics nightmares, while Traffic Road and Escape Road 2 are all about weaving through traffic without crashing. Prefer something calmer? Slow Roads is an endless, relaxing drive through procedurally generated scenery — great for unwinding. Whatever your mood, all of them are free and ready to play right now.
