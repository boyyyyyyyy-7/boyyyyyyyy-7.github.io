Launch & Smash

You're a luchador wrestler launching through Candyland. The goal? Smash through gummy bears and travel as far as possible. Launch from the ring, bounce off enemies, break through obstacles, and use momentum to keep flying.

It's a launcher game with RPG progression. Each run earns money. Money buys upgrades. Upgrades make you go farther. The loop is incredibly addictiveâ€”one more run, one more upgrade, one more record distance.

Momentum & Combos

Momentum is everything. Bouncing on special gummy bears launches you higher. Breaking through gates gives speed boosts. Hitting the ground slows you down. Master the timing of slams and bounces to maintain speed.

Combos multiply your earnings. Chain bounces together without touching the ground. The longer the combo, the more cash per enemy. This cash funds better upgrades, which enable longer runs.
