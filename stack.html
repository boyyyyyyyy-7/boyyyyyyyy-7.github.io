About Stack

🎮 Perfect Stacking Challenge

Blocks slide across the screen. Tap to drop them. If they land perfectly on the previous block, they stack. Miss the alignment and the overhanging part gets sliced off, making the next block smaller.

Perfect stacks are satisfying. Each precise drop builds the tower higher. But one bad placement shrinks your platform, making future placements exponentially harder. The game rewards precision and punishes rushing.

🎯 Rhythm & Timing

The blocks slide in a predictable pattern. Learning the rhythm is key. Some players count " the beats, others feel it visually. Either way, consistency wins over random tapping.

As your tower grows, blocks slide faster. The timing window shrinks. What worked at level 5 feels too slow at level 50. You need to adapt your rhythm as difficulty increases.

💡 High Tower Strategy

Watch the rhythm. Blocks slide at a consistent speed. Time your taps to this rhythm rather than reacting visually. As the tower grows taller, the blocks slide faster—adjust your timing accordingly.

Don't panic when the platform gets smaller. One perfect stack resets your block size to full width. Focus on getting that perfect drop rather than accepting mediocre placements.
