About Speed Stars

üéÆ Rhythm Running

Speed Stars is a running game where timing is everything. You're tapping to build speed and momentum, hitting perfect rhythm windows to accelerate faster. Miss the timing and you slow down. Nail it consistently and you become unstoppable. It's simple mechanically but incredibly satisfying when you hit your groove.

The appeal is in the flow state. When you're hitting every timing window perfectly, your runner just flies down the track. The visual feedback reinforces every good tap. The audio cues help you find the rhythm. It feels less like a game and more like performing.

üéØ Timing & Speed

Tap or click when the timing indicator hits the perfect zone. Early or late taps still move you forward, but perfect timing gives massive speed boosts. String together perfect taps and you'll unlock even faster speeds through combo bonuses.

Track layouts introduce obstacles and turns that break your rhythm. You need to adapt your timing to navigate corners without losing speed. Some sections demand faster taps, others slower. Reading the track ahead lets you prepare for timing changes.

üí° Perfect Runs

Focus on consistency over perfection initially. Getting 80% good taps beats going for 100% and missing half. As you internalize the rhythm, your accuracy naturally improves. Muscle memory handles timing once you've practiced enough.

Use audio cues if available. Visual timing is fine but adding audio creates a stronger rhythm anchor. And don't panic when you miss a perfect window‚Äîone miss doesn't ruin your run. Recover your rhythm and keep going.
