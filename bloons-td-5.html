About Bloons TD 5

Classic Tower Defense Action

Deploy a vast array of specialized monkey towers to pop every single invading bloon! Bloons TD 5 is widely considered one of the best tower defense games ever made, featuring deep strategy, tons of maps, and satisfying progression.

From the simple Dart Monkey to the devastating Super Monkey, every tower has multiple upgrade paths and special abilities. Mix and match your defense to handle different types of bloons including lead, camo, and regrow.

Infinite Strategic Mastery

Strategic placement is everything. Use Monkey Apprentices to slow enemies, Boomerang Throwers for curved paths, and Monkey Snipers for long-range coverage. Every map presents a new puzzle to solve.

Unlock specialized buildings, complete daily challenges, and rank up to gain permanent bonuses. With hundreds of rounds to conquer, the tactical possibilities are endless.

Popping Tips

Build a diverse defense to handle specialized bloons—you'll need "Camo Detection" for invisible bloons and "Sharp Shots" or fire for lead-coated ones. Don't forget about the Monkey Village to boost surrounding towers!

Save up for powerful abilities to clear the screen during chaotic late-game rounds. A well-timed "Maelstrom" can be the literal difference between life and leak!
