What are .io games?

".io games" are quick, competitive multiplayer games — named after the .io web domain where the genre took off with hits like Agar.io and Paper.io. The idea is simple: drop into a shared arena, grow bigger or outlast everyone else, and climb the leaderboard. Rounds are short and there's no setup, which makes them ideal for a few minutes of fun between classes.

Want territory control? Paper.io 2 is all about claiming the map without getting cut off. Feeling destructive? Hole.io lets you swallow an entire city, and Smash Karts turns it into a kart battle royale. For shooters, 1v1.lol and Shell Shockers bring the build-and-blast and egg-FPS chaos.
