A quieter bar.

Thin. Translucent. Light typography. No bold accent colors competing for attention — the site's content does the talking, the bar just holds the navigation.

What changed

  • 52px tall instead of 64px — sits lighter on every page
  • Frosted glass background (heavy backdrop blur) like macOS menus
  • Inter / SF Pro at 13.5px, regular weight — no uppercase shouting
  • Active page is a subtle white tint, not a colored underline
  • Streak and freeze chips are quiet — just text + small emoji
  • Single hairline border at the bottom, no gradient seams
  • Logo is a tiny 22px bordered "BG" — minimal, not a centerpiece

If you want to push further
