Why clicker games are perfect for class

Clicker and idle games (also called "incremental" games) are about one addictive loop: do a little, earn a little, spend it on upgrades that earn even more. They demand almost no attention span, save your progress automatically, and reward you just for checking back — which is exactly why they thrive on slow school days. There's nothing to download and nothing to set up.

Cookie Clicker is the genre's icon — bake cookies, buy grandmas, watch the numbers explode. Spacebar Clicker and Chill Guy Clicker put fun spins on the formula, while the Learn to Fly series and Duck Life 3 mix in training and progression between runs. All free, all unblocked, all ready instantly.
