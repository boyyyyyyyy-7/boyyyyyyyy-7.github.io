About Sand Game

Physics Sandbox Simulation

A physics sandbox where sand, water, fire, and various elements interact realistically. Pour sand and watch it pile up naturally. Add water and see how it flows and erodes the sand. Introduce fire and watch materials burn and react.

It's not about winning—it's about experimenting. Create dams that break under water pressure. Build structures and watch physics tear them down. Mix elements to see unexpected reactions.

Realistic Element Interactions

Every element behaves according to real physics. Sand flows like grains, settling into realistic piles. Water seeks the lowest point, eroding materials in its path. Fire spreads and consumes flammable substances.

The complexity emerges from combinations. Oil floats on water. Salt dissolves. Plant life grows in the right conditions. The more you experiment, the more interactions you discover.

Creative Experiments

Layer different materials to see how they interact. Sand and water create interesting erosion patterns. Fire spreads through flammable materials in realistic ways. Use walls to contain liquids and create complex structures.

Try creating volcanoes with lava, lakes with dams, or forests that can ignite. The sandbox is your canvas—physics is your paint.
