Hole.io

About Hole.io

🎮 Swallow Everything

You're a black hole moving around a city. Everything smaller than you falls in—trash cans, lamp posts, cars, people, eventually entire buildings. The more you consume, the bigger you grow. Bigger holes eat smaller holes. Simple concept, wildly addictive execution.

The multiplayer chaos makes it intense. Other players are trying to do the same thing. You're racing against the clock and against each other to become the biggest hole before time runs out.

🎯 Growth Strategy

Start with small objects. Don't waste time chasing things you can't swallow yet. Once you hit a size threshold, you can suddenly consume cars. Then street signs. Then buildings. Growth accelerates fast if you target the right objects.

Hunt other players when you're bigger than them. They're worth massive points. But stay away from bigger holes or you become their snack.

💡 Win More Matches

Dense areas have more small objects—perfect for early growth. Once you're mid-sized, move to areas with cars and larger items for faster expansion. Don't get greedy chasing one huge object when you could be vacuuming up dozens of smaller ones.

Watch the leaderboard. If you're dominating, play safe. If you're behind, take risks hunting bigger players or going for risky areas with large objects.
