üí° Survival Tips

Communication is key if you're playing with voice chat. Call out page locations. Warn teammates when you see movement. Information sharing dramatically improves survival odds compared to everyone going silent.

Don't run everywhere. Sprinting drains stamina. Save it for when you actually need to escape. Walking conserves energy while still covering ground. And if you hear footsteps that aren't yours? Time to run.
