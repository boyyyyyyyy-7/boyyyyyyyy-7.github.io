Raft Wars

About Raft Wars

🎮 Turn-Based Water Combat

You and your brother found treasure. Now pirates, vikings, and other enemies want it. Defend your treasure by launching tennis balls, grenades, and rockets from your raft. Aim, adjust power, and fire. Knock enemies into the water to win.

It's physics-based combat with progression. Each level brings tougher enemies with better weapons. You earn money to upgrade your shots, add team members, and unlock new ammo types. Simple concept, addictive execution.

🎯 Trajectory & Power

Hitting targets requires mastering angle and power. Too much power and you overshoot. Too little and you splash short. Wind affects trajectory. Distance matters.

Different weapons have different arcs. Grenades bounce and explode. Rockets fly straight and fast. Tennis balls arc high. Learning which weapon suits each situation is key to efficient victories.

💡 Winning Battles

Aim for direct hits to knock enemies off rafts in one shot. If you can't hit directly, aim near them—close explosions still do damage. Prioritize dangerous enemies first.

Upgrade your crew size early. More team members mean more shots per turn, which means faster victories and less damage taken. Better offense is better defense.
